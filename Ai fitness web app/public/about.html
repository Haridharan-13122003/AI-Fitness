About AI Fitness Trainer

Our Mission

Our goal is to make professional fitness guidance accessible to everyone. By leveraging simple AI rules and personalized data input, we aim to provide actionable, easy-to-understand fitness plans for people of all experience levels.

How it Works

  1. Register & Login: Create an account securely.
  2. Provide Details: Use the BMI calculator and Profile tab to input your height, weight, age, and fitness goals.
  3. Get Your Plan: Read your personalized workout and diet suggestions based on your data.
  4. Track & Chat: Record your daily progress and chat with our mock AI for instant motivational advice.
